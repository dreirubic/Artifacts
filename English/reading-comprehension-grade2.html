🐭 The Clever Little Mouse

Once upon a time, in a big, leafy forest, there lived a tiny mouse named Miko. Miko had big round ears, a long wiggly tail, and bright little eyes.

One day, Miko was walking home when he saw a huge piece of yellow cheese on the ground. "Oh wow! That looks delicious!" he squeaked happily.

But before he could take a bite, a large lion jumped out from behind a tree! "That cheese is mine!" roared the lion. His voice shook the leaves!

Miko was scared, but he was also very clever. He smiled at the lion and said, "Mr. Lion, that cheese is not yours — it fell from a farmer's cart. But I know where the farmer keeps MORE cheese! I can show you... if you are my friend."

The lion thought for a moment. Then he smiled and said, "Okay, little mouse. Let us be friends."

Miko and the lion became the best of friends. They shared food, played in the forest, and helped each other every day. And from that day on, no one ever bothered Miko again — because everyone knew he was friends with the lion! 🦁💛🐭

🐭 Ang Matalinong Munting Daga

Noong unang panahon, sa isang malaki at makulay na kagubatan, may naninirahan na isang munting daga na nagngangalang Miko. Si Miko ay may malaki at bilugang tainga, mahabang buntot, at maliit na maliwanag na mga mata.

Isang araw, habang pauwi si Miko, nakakita siya ng isang malaking piraso ng dilaw na keso sa lupa. "Ay naku! Mukhang masarap iyon!" bulong niya nang may saya.

Ngunit bago pa man siya makakagat, isang malaking leon ang lumabas mula sa likod ng puno! "Akin ang keso na iyan!" angal ng leon. Nanginig ang mga dahon sa lakas ng kanyang boses!

Natakot si Miko, ngunit siya ay napakatalino. Ngumiti siya sa leon at sinabi, "Ginoo Leon, hindi iyan sa inyo — nahulog iyan mula sa kariton ng isang magsasaka. Pero alam ko kung saan nagtitago ang magsasaka ng MARAMING keso! Ipapakita ko sa inyo... kung magiging kaibigan ninyo ako."

Nag-isip sandali ang leon. Pagkatapos ay ngumiti siya at sinabi, "Sige, munting daga. Maging magkaibigan tayo."

Si Miko at ang leon ay naging pinakamahusay na magkaibigan. Nagbabahagi sila ng pagkain, naglalaro sa kagubatan, at nagtutulungan araw-araw. At mula noon, walang nang nangggulo kay Miko — dahil alam ng lahat na kaibigan niya ang leon! 🦁💛🐭
