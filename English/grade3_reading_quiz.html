The Boy Who Forgot His Umbrella

Every morning, Lucas's mom reminded him to bring his umbrella to school. "It might rain today," she would say. But Lucas always forgot. He was too busy thinking about playing with his friends.

One Tuesday, dark clouds filled the sky when school ended. Lucas walked out with his classmates and looked up. Before he could run back inside, the rain began to pour down hard.

His classmate Maya was standing nearby, holding a big yellow umbrella. She saw Lucas getting wet and walked over to him. "We can share," she said with a smile. Together, they walked to the school gate, both staying dry under Maya's umbrella.

The next morning, Lucas packed his umbrella first before anything else. He even put a small sticker on it that said "REMEMBER!" so he would never forget again. He also decided that the next time someone needed help, he would be the one to share.

🔵 Part A: Choose the best answer.
1. Why did Lucas always forget his umbrella?
2. What color was Maya's umbrella?
3. On what day did it rain heavily after school?
4. What did Lucas put on his umbrella the next morning?
✅ Part B: True or False?
5. Lucas's mom never reminded him to bring his umbrella.
6. Maya shared her umbrella with Lucas.
7. Lucas and Maya got very wet walking home.
8. Lucas decided to help others the next time someone needed it.
✏️ Part C: Answer in your own words.
9. Why is it important to listen to your parents' reminders? Write 2 sentences.
10. Have you ever helped a friend like Maya helped Lucas? What did you do?
Tagalog · Grade 3 na Pagbabasa
Ang Punong Mangga ni Lolo

Sa likod ng bahay ni Amara ay may malaking punong mangga. Itinatanim iyon ng kanyang lolo noong bata pa siya. Bawat tag-init, nagbubunga ng maraming mangga ang puno at nagagalak ang buong pamilya.

Isang hapon, napansin ni Amara na may malalim na sugat sa puno. Tinawag niya ang kanyang lolo at itinanong kung ano ito. "Tinanggal ng ating kapitbahay ang isang malaking sanga para hindi mahulog sa kanilang bubong," paliwanag ng lolo nang may lungkot sa mukha.

Naawa si Amara. Naisip niya na gawin ang isang bagay para tulungan ang puno. Pumunta siya sa tindahan kasama ang kanyang nanay at bumili ng espesyal na gamot para sa puno. Maingat niyang nilagyan ng gamot ang sugat at tinakpan ito ng tela.

Pagkatapos ng ilang linggo, nagpasalamat si Lolo kay Amara. "Dahil sa iyong pagmamahal at pag-aalaga, lalago ulit ang ating puno," sabi niya. Ngumiti si Amara. Natuto siya na ang pagmamahal sa kalikasan ay isang uri ng pagmamahal sa pamilya.

🔴 Bahagi A: Piliin ang pinakamabuting sagot.
1. Sino ang nagtanim ng punong mangga?
2. Bakit may sugat ang punong mangga?
3. Saan pumunta si Amara para makakuha ng gamot sa puno?
4. Ano ang natutunan ni Amara sa kwentong ito?
✅ Bahagi B: Tama o Mali?
5. Nagbubunga ng mangga ang puno tuwing tag-init.
6. Masaya ang lolo nang makita ang sugat ng puno.
7. Tinakpan ni Amara ng tela ang sugat ng puno.
8. Ang lolo ang bumili ng gamot para sa puno.
✏️ Bahagi C: Sagutin sa sariling salita.
9. Bakit mahalaga ang pag-aalaga ng mga halaman o puno? Sumulat ng 2 pangungusap.
10. Mayroon ka bang paboritong puno o halaman sa inyong bakuran? Ilarawan ito.
