📖 Keyword Reference — Operations

Every verbal phrase has a keyword that tells you which operation (+, −, ×, ÷) to use. Memorize these and translating becomes easy!

➕ Addition
sum, plus, added to, increased by, more than, total, exceeds by, greater than
e.g. "7 more than n" → n + 7
➖ Subtraction
difference, minus, less than, subtracted from, decreased by, take away, reduced by
e.g. "p decreased by 4" → p − 4
✖️ Multiplication
product, times, multiplied by, twice (×2), thrice (×3), of, double
e.g. "twice x" → 2x
➗ Division
quotient, divided by, divided into, ratio of, split into equal parts, per
e.g. "n divided by 5" → n/5
⚠️ Watch out for subtraction order!
"10 less than x" → x − 10 (NOT 10 − x)
"m subtracted from 8" → 8 − m (NOT m − 8)
