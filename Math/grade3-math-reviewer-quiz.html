⚡ Important Formulas

🟩 Area of Rectangle
A = L × W
Length × Width
Answer: m² or cm²
⬛ Area of Square
A = s × s
Side × Side
All sides are equal!
📏 Perimeter of Rectangle
P = 2 × (L + W)
Add all four sides!
Answer: m or cm
🔷 Perimeter of Square
P = 4 × s
Multiply one side by 4!
All sides are the same.

🟩 Area — "How much space does it cover?"

📌 What is Area?

Area tells us how much surface is covered. Imagine tiling a floor — the number of tiles you need is the area! Area is always measured in square units like cm² or m².
📝 Example 1: A room is 5 m long and 3 m wide. What is its area?
→ A = L × W = 5 × 3 = 15 m²

📝 Example 2: A garden is 8 m long and 4 m wide. What is its area?
→ A = 8 × 4 = 32 m²

📝 Example 3: A square has a side of 6 cm. What is its area?
→ A = 6 × 6 = 36 cm²

📏 Perimeter — "How far is it around?"

📌 What is Perimeter?

Perimeter is the total distance around a shape. Imagine walking around a garden — the total distance you walk is the perimeter! Measured in m or cm (not square!).
📝 Example 1: A park is 10 m long and 6 m wide. What is its perimeter?
→ P = 2 × (10 + 6) = 2 × 16 = 32 m

📝 Example 2: A square has a side of 5 m. What is its perimeter?
→ P = 4 × 5 = 20 m

📝 Example 3: A room is 9 m long and 7 m wide. What is the perimeter?
→ P = 2 × (9 + 7) = 2 × 16 = 32 m
💡 Trick! To find perimeter, add ALL sides together!
Rectangle: top + bottom + left + right = L + W + L + W = 2L + 2W

📐 Measuring Units

📌 Length Units

We use different units to measure length! cm for small things (like a pencil), m for bigger things (like a room), and km for very long distances (like a road).

💡 Word Problem Tips

🔑 Key Words to Watch For

🟩 AREA Keywords
"covers" • "surface"
"how much space"
"tiles needed"
→ Use A = L × W
📏 PERIMETER Keywords
"around" • "fence"
"border" • "frame"
"how far to walk"
→ Use P = 2(L+W)
📝 Word Problem Example:
"A farmer wants to put a fence around his rectangular field that is 12 m long and 8 m wide."
→ "Fence" = PERIMETER → P = 2 × (12 + 8) = 2 × 20 = 40 m

"A painter needs to paint the floor of a room that is 6 m long and 4 m wide."
→ "Floor / paint" = AREA → A = 6 × 4 = 24 m²

🔍 Finding a Missing Side

📌 How to Find Missing Length or Width

If you know the Area and one side, divide to find the missing side!
→ Missing side = Area ÷ Known side

If you know the Perimeter and one side, work backwards!
→ Half of Perimeter = L + W, then subtract to find missing side.
📝 Example 1 (Area given):
Area = 24 m², Width = 4 m. Find the Length.
→ Length = 24 ÷ 4 = 6 m

📝 Example 2 (Perimeter given):
Perimeter = 20 m, Length = 6 m. Find the Width.
→ L + W = 20 ÷ 2 = 10 → W = 10 - 6 = 4 m
