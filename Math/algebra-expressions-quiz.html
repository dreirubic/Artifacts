📐 Algebraic Expressions

Grade 7 Math  •  Translating Phrases to Algebra

📖 Quick Review

An algebraic expression uses numbers, variables (letters), and operation symbols to represent a value. To translate a verbal phrase into an algebraic expression, look for keywords that tell you which operation to use.

Key Words by Operation
Operation Keywords / Phrases Example Expression
+ Addition sum, plus, added to, increased by, more than, total, exceeds by n + 7
− Subtraction difference, minus, less than, subtracted from, decreased by, take away, reduced by 8 − m
× Multiplication product, times, multiplied by, twice (×2), thrice (×3), of 7b
÷ Division quotient, divided by, divided into, ratio of, split into equal parts a/7
⚠️ Subtraction Order Matters!
"1 000 less than f" → f − 1 000  (NOT 1 000 − f)
"10 subtracted from c" → c − 10
