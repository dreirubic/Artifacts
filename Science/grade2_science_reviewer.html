🏔️ A. Landforms
🌐Landforms are the natural shapes of land found on Earth's surface.
⛰️Mountain – very high land. It is the highest landform. Example: Mayon Volcano.
🏕️Hill – high land, but shorter than a mountain. It has a rounded top.
🌾Plain – flat and low land. Good for farming and building homes.
🏜️Plateau – flat land that is HIGH up. Like a table on top of the Earth.
🏝️Island – land completely surrounded by water. The Philippines is made of islands!
🌄Valley – low land between mountains or hills. Rivers often flow through valleys.

🌊 B. Water Forms
💧Water forms are bodies of water found on Earth.
🌊Ocean – the largest body of saltwater. Example: Pacific Ocean.
🏖️Sea – smaller than an ocean. Also saltwater.
🏞️Lake – a body of freshwater surrounded by land on all sides.
🌊River – flowing water that moves from mountains to the sea.
💦Stream – a small, narrow river.
🌊Bay – a part of the sea that goes into the land. Example: Manila Bay.
🌊Gulf – a large bay. Example: Leyte Gulf.

🌿 C. Uses of Land and Water Forms
🌾Plains – farming (rice, corn, vegetables)
🏠Plains & Hills – building homes and communities
⛷️Mountains – hiking, mining, and getting fresh water from rivers that start there
🐟Oceans, Seas & Rivers – fishing, swimming, and transportation (boats)
🚢Bays & Ports – docking ships and ferries
💧Lakes & Rivers – source of fresh drinking water
⭐ Remember: Humans depend on BOTH land and water forms to live!
♻️ D. Renewable and Nonrenewable Resources
🔄Renewable Resources – resources that can be replaced or never run out. They are always available!
☀️Examples of renewable: Sunlight, Wind, Water (rain), Plants, Animals
⛽Nonrenewable Resources – resources that CANNOT be replaced once used up. We must use them wisely!
🛢️Examples of nonrenewable: Coal, Oil (petroleum), Natural Gas, Minerals (gold, silver, copper)

💚 E. Care and Concern for Earth
🌱Reduce – use less of things. Don't waste resources.
🔁Reuse – use things again instead of throwing them away.
♻️Recycle – turn used things into something new (like paper, plastic, cans).
🌳Plant trees – trees give us oxygen and help stop floods.
🚫Do NOT litter – throwing trash on land or in water harms animals and our environment.
💡Save electricity – turn off lights when not in use. Use less power!
🚿Save water – don't leave the faucet running. Water is precious!
⭐ The 3 R's: REDUCE 🌱 · REUSE 🔁 · RECYCLE ♻️ – We can all help take care of our Earth!
