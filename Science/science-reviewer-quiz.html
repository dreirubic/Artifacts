Lessons 4–6: Heat Energy

☀️ What is Heat?

Heat is a form of energy that keeps us warm. It is very important in our everyday life. Heat can cook our food, and do many other useful things.
The SUN is the main source of heat and light on Earth. When sunlight hits a surface, it is absorbed and converted into heat!

🌡️ Other Sources of Heat

Besides the sun, there are many other things that give off heat:

🕯️ Candle
burning flame
🪨 Burning Charcoal
charcoal fire
🔥 Burning Wood
campfire / fireplace
⚡ Electric Appliances
oven, flat iron, kettle
⛽ Burning Gas
gas stove
🏃 The Human Body
our bodies make heat too!

🍳 Uses of Heat

Heat is used in many ways in our daily life:

👕 Flat Iron
press clothes
🍲 Gas Stove
cooking
💧 Electric Kettle
boiling water
🐣 Incubator
body warming
🔥 Fireplace
warm newborn babies
📡 Microwave Oven
food warming

🌿 How Plants, Animals & People Use Heat

🌱 Plants need heat from the sun to grow. Seeds only start to grow when there is warmth from the sun.
🦎 Some animals bask in the sun to keep their bodies warm. They depend on the sun's heat to function normally.
👨‍👩‍👧 People use heat from the sun to dry clothes, and also use solar panels to convert the sun's heat into electricity!

⚠️ Too Much Heat = Harmful!

❌ Too much heat IS harmful to the body! It can cause burns, dehydration, and other health problems. Always be careful around heat sources.
✅ The right amount of heat keeps us warm, cooks our food, and helps plants and animals survive.

Lesson 2: Living & Non-living Things

🧬 What are Living Things?

Living things are things that are alive. They grow, eat, breathe, move, and reproduce (make more of themselves)!

🌱 Living Things

  • 🐱 Cat
  • 🐶 Dog
  • 🌸 Flowers
  • 🌳 Trees
  • 🦋 Insects
  • 🐄 Cow
  • 🐴 Horse
  • 🐑 Sheep
  • 👨 People
  • 🍄 Mushrooms

🪑 Non-living Things

  • 🪑 Chair
  • 📚 Book
  • 🪟 Table
  • 🥛 Glass
  • 📄 Paper
  • 🍽️ Plate
  • ✏️ Pencil
  • 🪨 Rock/Soil
  • 🏠 House

🔑 How to Tell If Something is Living

🌱 It GROWS
🍽️ It EATS / GETS FOOD
💨 It BREATHES
🏃 It can MOVE
👶 It can REPRODUCE
💧 It NEEDS WATER

🌻 Why are Plants Important?

🥗 Plants are important sources of food — like okra, eggplant, tomato, and chili.
💊 Some plants are used for medicine — like oregano and laguno/saluyot.
🌺 Some plants are used for decoration — like bougainvillea and sampaguita.
🪑 Plants make soil rich with nutrients. They are also used to make furniture, houses, and clothing materials.

🌍 Living & Non-living Things in the Garden

In a garden, you can find BOTH living and non-living things. Soil and rocks are non-living. Plants, insects, and other animals are living.
💡 Non-living things like soil are still very important! Plants are planted in soil. Insects live among rocks. Both help the garden thrive.
