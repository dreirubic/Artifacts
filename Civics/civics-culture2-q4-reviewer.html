Week 1
🧒 Children's Rights

Every child has rights — these are things you deserve just because you are a child!

🍎 Right to FoodChildren have the right to eat healthy food every day.
🏠 Right to a HomeChildren have the right to a safe and comfortable place to live.
🏫 Right to EducationChildren have the right to go to school and learn.
🏥 Right to HealthChildren have the right to see a doctor and be healthy.
🎮 Right to PlayChildren have the right to rest and play.
💛 Right to Love & CareChildren have the right to be loved and cared for by their family.
🛡️ Right to ProtectionChildren have the right to be safe from harm.
⭐ Remember: Rights are for ALL children — no matter where you come from or what you look like!
Week 2
🤝 My Duties & Responsibilities

Along with rights, we also have duties — things we should do to help others and our community.

🏡 At HomeHelp with chores, obey your parents, take care of your siblings.
🏫 At SchoolListen to your teacher, study hard, be kind to classmates.
🌳 In the CommunityFollow rules, keep surroundings clean, respect others.
🗑️ Keep CleanThrow garbage in the trash. Don't litter!
📖 Study HardLearning is your job as a student.
🙏 Respect EldersBe polite and kind to older people.
⭐ Remember: Rights and duties go together! When everyone does their duty, everyone can enjoy their rights. 🌈
Week 3
🏛️ Government Services to the Community

The government helps the community by providing important services for everyone.

🏥 Health ServicesHospitals and health centers help sick people get well.
🏫 EducationThe government builds public schools for children to study.
🚒 Fire StationFirefighters protect us from fires.
🚓 Police StationPolice officers keep us safe and follow the law.
🛣️ Roads & BridgesThe government builds roads so people can travel.
💧 Water & ElectricityThe government helps provide clean water and power.
📮 Post OfficeHelps deliver letters and packages.
🌳 ParksGovernment makes parks where people can rest and play.
⭐ Remember: The government works for all the people. Its job is to help and protect the community! 🇵🇭
Weeks 4–5
🌟 Cooperation in the Community

Cooperation means working together to help each other. When we cooperate, we can do more!

🤝 BayanihanA Filipino tradition of helping each other as a community.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 FamilyFamily members help each other with chores and decisions.
🏫 SchoolClassmates work together in group activities and projects.
🌆 CommunityNeighbors help each other during difficult times.
💬 CommunicationTalk and listen to each other kindly.
🎯 Common GoalWorking together to reach the same goal.
⭐ Remember: "Sama-sama, mas magaan!" — Together, things become easier! The spirit of Bayanihan makes our community strong. 💪
